You will learn simple C++ programming to understand basic tools like "Variable", "Loop", "Function" and to make simple algorithms from them.

About Prof. Notreal Bing

Biography

Prof. Notreal Bing received his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Ottwa University of Science and Techonology in 2004. He continued his Master of Science in Data Science from the same universitiy and then completed his study of Doctor of Philosophy in Information Technology from Ontario Technological University in 2015.

After Ph.D, he contributed to Visualize where he developed algorithms and techniques in the emerging field of data visualization. After two years of his service to Visualize, he joined Awerence University in 2018 and has served as a Fellow of Data Science of Canada since Feburary 2018.
